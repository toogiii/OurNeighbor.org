Ways to Help

Materials

Our Neighbor is in constant need of materials & tools in order to keep our residences in tip-top shape. Especially useful contributions could include: gently used furniture, clothing, or household items. We are currently in need of washers, dryers, and dishwashers at this time. This list will be updated regularly as our needs evolve. Any ideas or suggestions are always appreciated; please contact us.

Financial Support

As a 501 (c) (3), Our Neighbor is in constant need of financial support, both from private individuals & from businesses. If you or your company would like to help, please contact Mary Margaret Calvert at (770) 535-1000 or by donating online. All donations are fast, easy, and tax-deductable!

The North Georgia Community Foundation currently manages more  than 50 agency funds, including ours, for various nonprofits! Working with the Foundation offers a cost-effective investment option and frees our staff from time consuming administrative and investment responsibilities so we can remain focused on our core mission. The Community Foundation exists as a community savings account making sure that a donor's gift will do good work, now and in the future. Donate to the Community Foundation and you will ensure that Our Neighbor Inc. goes on!

Volunteer

One great way to help Our Neighbor is with your time. There are many volunteer opportunities:
Maintenance & Repair: If you have maintenance or repair skills, whether professional or simply from working on your own house, we need you! From simple repairs and touching up paint to helping revitalize our houses, there's almost always something to be done.

Meals & Food Provide a meal or share one with our residents!

At our associated bookstore, The Next Chapter, we take book donations every week day from 11am to 5pm.
