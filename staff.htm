T A M M Y
Tammy is a Next Chapter Bookstore Manager and is also a caregiver for Our Neighbor Inc. Tammy's has many talents. but her best talent given from God is the ability to listen to others when they need to talk. Her traits include: caring, loving and motherly. When asked what led her to work for ONI, her answer was that she wanted to help make a change in the community for disabled as well as she was already a caregiver to a resident moving in to ONI. Her favorite things about her job is: seeing the smiles on the residents and the employees faces when they come to work. she also loves the generous nature of the customers and of course like everyone, the love of books. Tammy is also a registered nurse for Texas.


R E N E E
My name is Renee and is also a Next Chapter Bookstore Manager. I enjoy fishing, reading, working out at the gym, running/walking 5ks, and horseback riding; in fact, I collect anything horse related. I also collect crosses with bible verses. I have Cerebral Palsy in the right side of my body, along with a speech impairment. I am a very easy-going, flexible, and dependable individual. I like working at NCB because I enjoy the computer-work, as well as the people whom I connect with, and of course, books.
