Annual Giant Book Sale

Our annual GINORMUS book sale is held on the second weekend in October, the same weekend and hours as Mule Camp. The preview sale is Thursday during mule camp setup ($5 admission), with the general sale running Friday through Sunday (FREE admission), 10AM to 6PM daily.

In the months leading up to the sale, we ALWAYS need volunteers to help sort books. This is a GREAT opportunity for high school/college service hours. The location will be at 104 Main Street, in Gainesville. In addition to time, we often need certain items donated for preparation and/or for the sale itself such as long tables, portable and/or box fans and, of course, books!!!

“Like” us on Facebook for updates on when we are preparing for this event and how you may be able to help.
